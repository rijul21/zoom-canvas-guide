Using Zoom in Canvas: A Quick Start Guide for UF Students

This short guide will help you set up and use Zoom inside your Canvas course at the University of Florida. It covers the basics of joining, scheduling, and managing Zoom sessions for your classes.

Step 1: Access Zoom in Canvas

  1. Log in to UF e-Learning (Canvas).
  2. Choose your course from the dashboard.
  3. Click on the Zoom tab in the course navigation menu.

Step 2: Join a Zoom Meeting

  1. Find the scheduled session under the Upcoming Meetings tab.
  2. Click Join when it's time for class.

Step 3: Schedule a Meeting (Optional)

If your instructor allows you to schedule meetings (e.g., for group work):

  1. Click Schedule a New Meeting.
  2. Enter meeting details and click Save.
Accessibility Tip: Always turn on captions during meetings. You can find this under the Zoom toolbar > "CC".

Checklist Before Class

  • Test your audio and video
  • Join from a quiet place with good lighting
  • Mute your mic when not speaking
  • Use the Zoom chat for questions
